SELF E Hackathon 2023 (Top 5 Winners)

In my first Hackathon, hosted by the Department of Computer Science Engineering at Sri Eshwar College of Engineering, Coimbatore, from October 3 to 19, 2023, my team and I worked on a waste management problem. We created WasteX, a solution that uses both hardware and software to help manage waste better, raise awareness, and keep our city clean in a cost-effective way.

Out of 100 teams, we were among the top 32 chosen for the 6-hour Grand Finale. We were happy to be one of the top 5 winning teams and received cash prizes and trophies from the Principal. It was exciting to see our hard work pay off in my first Hackathon. My teammates were Nithyasarathi Mhithun and Karthikesh.

CodeFiesta 2024 (Top 3 Winners)

Codefiesta was a 6-hour contest held on January 4, 2024, at Sri Eshwar College of Engineering. Each team was tasked with building a model based on a given topic using the C language. Our team was assigned the development of a School Management System. We put in a lot of effort and successfully completed the project, securing third place in the contest. The project was evaluated by college professors and industry experts. I worked alongside with my talented teammates Kavin, Jameel Ahmed, and Surya Prabha.

Freshathon 2024 (Participation)

In Freahathon 2024, which took place from May to June 2024, I participated in a project expo exclusively for first-year students. The event, organized by the Department of Science and Humanities, featured four stages: Zeroth Review, First Review, Second Review, and Final Expo. My team, consisting of Veera Vasanthan, Surendhar, and me, developed a Waste Management and Waste Segregator project. We received valuable guidance and motivation from our mentors and evaluators, which was instrumental in the development of our project.

Despite our significant effort and dedication, we were awarded a participation certificate. The experience was invaluable, providing us with important insights and skills that will benefit us in future projects.
